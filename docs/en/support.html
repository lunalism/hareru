Support

Q1. What is "real spending"?

Hareru's core feature automatically excludes internal transfers (your account → your account) and shows only actual spending.

Q2. Can I link my bank account?

Bank and card auto-linking is not currently supported. All records are entered manually.

Q3. Where is my data stored?

All data is stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Q4. What features are free?

Expense/income/transfer recording, automatic internal transfer exclusion, budget management, category reports, and financial dictionary are all free.

Q5. What languages are supported?

Japanese, Korean, and English. The app automatically displays in your system language.

Q6. How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings and select "Reset Data" to remove all data. Uninstalling the app also removes all device data.
